Data Science · Machine Learning
Community Violent Crime Rate Prediction
Problem

Can socioeconomic factors predict community-level violent crime to support evidence-based policy?

Data

1,994 US communities, 127 features from the 1990 Census, LEMAS law enforcement survey, and 1995 FBI UCR crime data.

Method

Linear, quadratic & logistic regression; p-value feature selection; quadratic terms to capture non-linear poverty/crime relationships.

Impact

R²=0.907 (quadratic), AUC=0.962 (logistic); surfaced racial/socioeconomic bias embedded in model features via residential segregation patterns.

Sports Analytics · Data Visualization
NBA Shot Analysis — The Three-Point Revolution
Problem

Was the three-point explosion a Steph Curry effect, or did it signal a permanent structural shift across the league?

Data

NBA shot-tracking logs across all teams, 2004–2024, including Curry's injury-sidelined seasons as a natural control.

Method

R + ggplot2; time-series trend analysis; spatial shot-zone heatmaps; injury-season control analysis to isolate individual vs. systemic effect.

Impact

Three-point share nearly doubled (22% → 42%); rates stayed elevated through Curry's injury seasons — confirming a permanent league-wide strategic shift.
