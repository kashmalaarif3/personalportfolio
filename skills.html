Institute for Systems Biology

During my time as a Research Project Intern we focused on research that suggested it may be possible to eliminate the correlation between age and diseases. Overall, we worked to leverage systems biology approaches to understand the mechanistic links among the processes that accompany and/or lead to aging. In specfic we created research methods to look at the compartmentalization of proteins into non-membrane-bound organelles, which have been implicated in the regulation of a wide variety of cell processes ranging from skin barrier formation to T-cell receptor signal transduction.
